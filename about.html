What is What2Do?

Have you ever traveled to a different city and unsure of what was around you? Or perhaps searching for something new and exciting in your current city? If either of these people are you, then you're in the right place! What2Do allows you to dynamically search for anything your heart desires: a bite to eat, a national park to visit, a local bar to drown your sorrows in alcohol, and more!
